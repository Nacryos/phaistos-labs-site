# Visual asset sources

## Marginal creatures

The dragon, butterfly, and bird cutouts were created with an image model from the user's manuscript references and then locally chroma-keyed, cropped, and compressed. Reference files supplied in `Downloads`:

- `il_570xN.1685381798_46iu.avif`
- `CanticlesSpirals.png`
- `6C8021223-bill-m--finkD2BA78A8-C110-FC90-A2CD-BD96084CBA45.webp`

## Illuminated mission initial

The mission paragraph's decorated **A** is a cleaned cutout derived from the *Isny Gradual*, an anonymous parchment manuscript with neumes dated to around 1200. The source reproduction is marked as public domain on [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Isnyer_Graduale_c1200_Initiale_A.jpg). An image model isolated the historical letterform from its blue field; the result was then locally chroma-keyed, cropped, and compressed.

## Linear A sign drawings

The four faint Linear A marks are representative sign-occurrence drawings from [SigLA — The Signs of Linear A](https://sigla.phis.me/), by Ester Salgarella and Simon Castellan. SigLA's dataset and drawings are licensed under [CC BY-NC-SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/). The source drawings were already transparent PNGs; they were cropped, recolored, and compressed without substituting Unicode glyphs.

- AB01: `PH 31a_17`
- AB08: `ZA 10b_18`
- AB27: `HT 10a_8`
- AB31: `KH 5_11`

Retrieved 2026-07-13.

## Voynich gallows marks

The two faint gallows marks are direct crops from the user-supplied Voynich manuscript reference `6C8021223-bill-m--finkD2BA78A8-C110-FC90-A2CD-BD96084CBA45.webp`. Adjacent writing and the paper ground were removed locally while preserving the original ink strokes.
